This huge graph shows all the athletes participating in the Olympics 2016. However, it becomes clear that many of the athletes do not have any links to any other athlete. This is due to many athletes only having very little information on the Wikipedia page, and no link to any other athlete competing at the Olympics. Even though these athletes might be really interesting, we will move them from the graph, since Network science only makes sense if there is a network (e.g. nodes and links connected).
